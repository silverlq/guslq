About Me

I am an experienced software developer with geospatial training and a passion for innovation. My academic background includes an MSc in geography and a BSc in geology, both with a focus in programming.

My current day-to-day activities include full-stack development of geological data processes, including ingestion, ETL, and maintenance. I have previously worked with GIS software development and administration.

Some of my latest scientific publications involve remote sensing applied to forest mensuration including computer vision and machine learning procedures.

When not at work, I enjoy cycling, camping, stargazing and socializing with friends in lively sessions of board games. I sporadically participate in game-making marathons, having participated in nine such events since 2013.
